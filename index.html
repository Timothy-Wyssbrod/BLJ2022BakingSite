Welcome! On this page you will find my guide on how to bake Oatmeal Raisin Cookies.
Why Oatmeal Rasin you might ask? Cause Oatmeal Raisin slaps. And is pretty underappreciated

Step "0": The ingredients

For this recipe you don't really need much. Some ingredients are somewhat hard to comeby, but they won't cost you much.

Ingredients

  • 230 grams of butter

  • 200 grams of brown sugar

  • 50 grams of granulated sugar

  • 3 eggs

  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla sugar

  • 1 tablespoon of molasses

  • 180 grams of all-purpose flour

  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon

  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

  • 240 grams of oats

  • 140 grams of raisins

Step 1: Mixing the butter and sugar

This step is pretty simple. All you have to do is mix the butter with the sugar until you end up with a relatively fluffy consistancy.
Below you'll see how it looked on my end. Don't worry if the color differs. The consistancy has to be the same though.

Step 2: Adding the eggs

For this step I recommend you use a hand mixer to ease the process. You have to add the eggs and make sure they are thoroughly mixed along together with the butter.

Step 3: Adding the vanilla and molasses

Just add the molasses and vanilla sugar. What else do you want me to say. Mix it up well and you're good.

Step 4: Dry ingredients

Next whisk the flour, baking-soda, cinnamon and salt. That's it

Step 5: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ones

Mix it.

Step 6: Let the dough chill

Set the dough to chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

Step 7: Bake the cookies

Roll the cookies into cylindrical shapes and place them on a baking tray. Bake them at 180 degrees celcius for 10-15 minutes.
